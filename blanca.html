Blanca Lake

Home Summary of Hikes About me


Features of this hike:

  • This hike is much more difficult to get to, since the road to the trailhead is washed out. Hikers now start at the washout, extending their trip.
  • The color of the lake is a bright-light-seafoam green
  • If you jump in the lake, it will (probably literally) take your breath away. But it feels great on a hot day!
For more info on this hike, visit wta.org
