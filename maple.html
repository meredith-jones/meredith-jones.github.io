Maple Loop Pass

Home Summary of Hikes About me


Features of this hike:

  • This hike is best in the fall when the larches are at the peak of their golden color!
  • On a clear day, it features beautiful mountain views, including a great view of Mt. Baker.
  • There are multiple side-hikes you can venture out on.
  • Despite it's remote location, it tends to be quite popular, so get there early!!
For more info on this hike, visit wta.org
