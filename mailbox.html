Mailbox Peak

Home Summary of Hikes About me


Features of this hike:

  • This hike is a real butt-kicker, gaining over 1000 ft per mile!
  • The top features a mailbox with a sign-in book
  • There's a new trail that is longer (but less steep), but real hikers do the old trail! ;)
  • The peak offers 360 views! On a clear day you can see out to Seattle, Rainier, and more!
For more info on this hike, visit wta.org
