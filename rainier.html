Spary Park

Home Summary of Hikes About me


Features of this hike:

  • You'll have epic views of Mt. Rainier along the way.
  • If you go at the right time, you'll see wildflowers galore in Spray Park.
  • You also get a great view of the valley on the other side of Spray Park.
  • You can take a quick offshoot hike to see Spray Falls.
  • The trail is well-maintained and pretty gentle, but the road to the trailhead is long and pothole-ridden.
For more info on this hike, visit wta.org
