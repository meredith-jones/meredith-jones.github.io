Goat Lake

Home Summary of Hikes About me


Features of this hike:

  • The journey itself is incredibly beautiful--it's along a creek with several falls.
  • Don't bother taking the upper trail--the lower trail is much nicer.
  • The lake with the mountain in the background is really cool!!
  • You probably won't see any goats there, FYI
For more info on this hike, visit wta.org
