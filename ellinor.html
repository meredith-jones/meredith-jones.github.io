Mount Ellinor

Home Summary of Hikes About me

Features of this hike:

  • This is another butt-kicker, though not as long as Mailbox Peak, it's about as steep.
  • You may see mountain goats on or near the trail.
  • On a clear day, the summit offers views of the olympic mountain range, the hood canal, and even out to Rainier.
  • This hike takes you through a couple of different and interesting types of terrain.
  • There's a lower and an upper trailhead. Take the upper one and save yourself 3 miles!
For more info on this hike, visit wta.org
