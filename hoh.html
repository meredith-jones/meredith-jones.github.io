Hoh River Trail

Home Summary of Hikes About me

Features of this hike:

  • This hike is in the US's only rainforest!
  • The dense forest is home to many interesting critters and plants
  • The hike is next the Hoh River, and you'll see a few waterfalls along the path.
  • The trail does have some major obstacles, such as climbing over gigantic fallen trees.
  • The air you breathe in the Hoh rainforest feels unlike any other.
For more info on this hike, visit wta.org
