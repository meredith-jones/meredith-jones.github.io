Lake Serene and Bridal Veil Falls

Home Summary of Hikes About me

Features of this hike:

  • This hike is fairly easy to get to--no miles of potholed-dirt road, which is a great relief.
  • Make the side trek to Bridal Veil Falls, and you won't be disappointed!
  • The lake itself is beautiful, and when you first round the corner and see the lake, you'll feel amazing.
  • You get a great view of Mt. Index!
  • You'll also get a great view of the Skykomish River.
For more info on this hike, visit wta.org
