Colchuck Lake

Home Summary of Hikes About me


Features of this hike:

  • There are a few beautiful stream crossings.
  • You get to pass through many different types of terrain.
  • The color of the lake is a beautiful teal.
  • In the background of the lake you can look up and see the daunting Dragontail Peak.
  • On the other side of the Peak is the coveted Enchantments, which makes for a great backpacking trip, but requires a permit.
  • The hike is not far from the German town of Leavenworth, a great place to stop for post-hike food and drinks.
For more info on this hike, visit wta.org
