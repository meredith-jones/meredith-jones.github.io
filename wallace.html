Home Summary of Hikes About me

Features of this hike:

  • This hike features 3 awesome waterfalls!
  • You can also extend your hike and visit Wallace Lake.
  • It's very easy to access, so it gets crowded. Be sure to get there early.
  • This hike is a great intro hike if you just moved to WA or have friends in town.
For more info on this hike, visit wta.org
